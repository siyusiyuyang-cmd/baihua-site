我们相信的 // WHAT WE BELIEVE IN

Baihua incubates original Chinese-language podcast projects and encourages free, inclusive and high quality discourse within the growing Chinese diaspora.

We believe in and are dedicated to growing original Chinese-language content and community that transcends borders, flourishes in spite of the threat of censorship, xenophobia, and misinformation, preserves and celebrates the joy of free discourse.

百花孵化原创中文播客项目，推动在不断壮大的全球华人社群中开展自由、包容且高质量的对话。我们致力于培育与发展跨越边界的中文原创内容与社区，使其在审查、排外与虚假信息的威胁之下依然茁壮成长，并守护、赞颂自由讨论的喜悦。

我们在做什么 // WHAT WE DO

In service of our mission, Baihua helps prospective creators launch their projects from scratch, from idea inception, branding, production to distribution and audience growth. We also provide consulting services to existing podcast creators about content strategy, production workflow and community engagement.

We aim to nurture and diversify the Chinese-language podcast scene, encourage quality discussions that enriches the Chinese diaspora community.

我们从零到一帮助创作者启动他们的项目：涵盖创意孵化、品牌打造、制作、分发与受众增长。我们也为现有播客提供咨询服务，包括内容策略、制作流程与社区运营。我们致力于培育并多元化中文播客生态，鼓励能够丰富华人社群的高质量讨论。
